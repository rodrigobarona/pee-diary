## 7. Data Visualization Style

Charts should:

- avoid sharp spikes
- avoid red/green contrasts

Preferred:

- Line charts with rounded joins
- Soft bar charts
- Time-based groupings

Never:

- Leaderboards
- Scores
- Aggressive streak pressure
